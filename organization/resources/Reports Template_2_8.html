Common Definitions

Term Definition
Application, Business Unit or Product A logical element or segment of a company (such as DevOps, Corporate DevOps, MIDAS etc.) representing a specific business function, and a definite place on the organizational chart, under the domain of a manager. Also, called department, division, or a functional area.
Company Information All materials or information in whatever form, whether written, oral, digital, or otherwise, (a) that is defined as "confidential” or a "trade secret” under any {{ organization.name }} Policy or under any agreement to which {{ organization.name }} is a party; (b) that is subject to special protections that require confidentiality under any law or regulation; (c) that is non-public and that relates to {{ organization.name }}'s finances, customers, employees, operations, research, development, facilities, or business, and (d) Customer Information
Confidential Information Shall mean certain non-public information acquired by employees in the course of employment which is treated as confidential by the Company or a Company client. It also includes any and all information entrusted by the Company in confidence by third parties, its patents and copyrights and any and all information defined as "trade secrets” under federal or state law.
Customer An individual or entity who is a consumer and with whom {{ organization.name }} has or had an ongoing customer relationship.
